How to Poach an Egg

Poaching an egg requires a gentle touch. Bring a pot of water to a simmer and add a splash of vinegar. Crack an egg into a small bowl. Create a gentle whirlpool in the water and carefully slide the egg into the center. Let it cook for about 3-4 minutes, or until the white is set and the yolk is still runny. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Serve on toast or with your favorite breakfast dish.

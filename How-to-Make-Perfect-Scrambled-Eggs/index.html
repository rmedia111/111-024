To make perfect scrambled eggs, start by whisking together eggs, salt, and a splash of milk or cream. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat and melt a small amount of butter. Pour in the egg mixture and let it sit for a moment. Gently stir with a spatula, lifting and folding the eggs until they are softly set and slightly runny in places. Remove from heat immediately as they will continue to cook in the residual heat. Serve warm with toast or your favorite breakfast sides.
